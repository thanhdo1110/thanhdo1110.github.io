Your Security Is Our Priority

We understand that API keys and certificates are sensitive. That's why CTDO Connect is built with security-first principles.

  • All API keys are stored securely in the iOS Keychain with hardware encryption
  • No data is ever sent to our servers - direct communication with Apple only
  • No analytics, tracking, or third-party SDKs that could compromise your data
  • App works completely offline after initial authentication
  • Open and transparent about our data practices
