Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we handle your data.

Last Updated: January 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to CTDO Connect ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our app.

üõ°Ô∏è Privacy First: CTDO Connect is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any external servers. All sensitive information stays on your device.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Unlike many apps, CTDO Connect is built to respect your privacy completely. We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports sent to external servers
  • Any data from your Apple Developer account

3. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

To provide our services, certain information is stored locally on your device:

3.1 App Store Connect API Keys

When you add an API key to the app, the following information is stored:

  • Issuer ID: Your App Store Connect team's Issuer ID
  • Key ID: The identifier of your API key
  • Private Key: The .p8 private key file contents

üîê Security: All API key data is stored exclusively in the iOS Keychain, which provides hardware-level encryption. This data is protected by your device's secure enclave and cannot be accessed by other apps or extracted from backups.

3.2 Cached Data

To improve performance, the app caches data retrieved from Apple's servers:

  • List of certificates, profiles, devices, and identifiers
  • This cached data is stored in the app's sandboxed storage
  • You can clear this cache at any time from the Settings screen

3.3 App Preferences

User preferences are stored locally using iOS UserDefaults:

  • Selected language (English/Vietnamese)
  • Background update preferences
  • Selected API key

4. How Your Data is Used

Your API keys are used solely to authenticate requests to Apple's App Store Connect API. The app:

  • Generates JWT tokens locally using your private key
  • Sends authenticated requests directly to Apple's servers (api.appstoreconnect.apple.com)
  • Displays the responses within the app interface

‚úÖ Direct Communication: All network requests go directly from your device to Apple's servers. We do not operate any intermediate servers, proxies, or data collection endpoints.

5. Third-Party Services

CTDO Connect communicates with the following third-party services:

5.1 Apple App Store Connect API

The app communicates with Apple's official API to manage your developer resources. Apple's privacy policy applies to this data exchange. Visit Apple's Privacy Policy for more information.

5.2 No Analytics or Advertising SDKs

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. This means:

  • No Google Analytics or Firebase
  • No Facebook SDK
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services (like Crashlytics)

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Keychain Storage: Sensitive data is stored in iOS Keychain with hardware encryption
  • HTTPS Only: All API communications use TLS 1.2 or higher encryption
  • No Cloud Sync: Your API keys are not synced to iCloud or any cloud service
  • Memory Safety: Sensitive data is cleared from memory when not in use

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data is retained until you manually delete it or uninstall the app
  • You can delete individual API keys from the Settings screen
  • You can clear all cached data from the Settings screen
  • Uninstalling the app removes all associated data from your device

8. Children's Privacy

CTDO Connect is designed for professional software developers. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The app is rated 4+ but requires an Apple Developer Program membership to be useful, which is only available to adults.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • For significant changes, displaying an in-app notification

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all data stored by the app at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete all data by clearing cache and removing API keys
  • Portability: Since data is on your device, you already have full access

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: support@ctdo.net
  • Website: https://ctdo.net
