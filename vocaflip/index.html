What This Means For You

Because VocaFlip operates entirely offline and stores all data locally on your device, there are no privacy choices to configure. You have complete control over your data by default.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I backup my data?
A: Your data is automatically included in iCloud device backups if enabled on your device.
Q: Can I transfer data to another device?
A: Use the Export feature to share your study sets as .vocaflip files, then import them on your new device.
Q: How do I import vocabulary?
A: VocaFlip supports multiple formats: TXT (word meaning per line), TSV, CSV, and JSON.
Q: Is there a limit on vocabulary sets?
A: No! Create unlimited folders and study sets with as many words as you need.
